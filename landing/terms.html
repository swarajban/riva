Description of Service

Riva is an AI-powered email scheduling assistant. The service:

  • Monitors a connected "assistant" Gmail account for scheduling requests
  • Analyzes email conversations to understand scheduling needs
  • Checks calendar availability
  • Proposes meeting times to participants
  • Sends confirmation requests via SMS or Telegram
  • Creates calendar events once confirmed

Riva requires you to connect a separate Google account (your "assistant account") that handles email and calendar operations. This is distinct from your personal Google account used for login.
